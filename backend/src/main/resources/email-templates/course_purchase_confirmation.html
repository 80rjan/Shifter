Tips to Maximize Your Learning Journey

  1. Explain what you learned to someone else The best way to check your understanding is to teach the concept. If you can explain it simply, you know it well.
  2. Eliminate distractions during study blocks. When you sit down to learn, silence your phone and close unnecessary tabs. Focused, shorter sessions are more effective than distracted, long ones.
  3. Take frequent, short breaks Stepping away from your screen every 45-60 minutes keeps your mind fresh and prevents burnout. Grab water or stretch!
  4. Take notes in your own words. Writing down key concepts helps your brain process and retain information much better than simply reading or listening.
